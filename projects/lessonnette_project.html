Lessonnette Project

Web Design | UI/UX Design | Interface Design | Mockups & Wireframes

Lessonnette Project is an interface project for an online academy that offers different complementary courses available to target audience wherever they are.

Lessonnette is an online education platform that specializes in offering educational videos related to design, development and digital marketing. Our focus is on providing high-quality content to people on a limited budget. We want to convey a sense of loyalty and, at the same time, be friendly and accessible to our users.

Project objectives:
1. Quality Educational Content:
- Create and curate educational videos taught by excellent instructors.
- Cover key areas such as graphic design, web development, programming, SEO, social media and marketing strategies.
2. Accessibility and Affordability:
- Offer an affordable experience for those looking to learn without spending large sums of money.
- Convey the idea that quality education should not be expensive.
3. Brand and Coherent Visual System:
- Design a solid brand that reflects trust and professionalism.
- Create an intuitive mobile interface that facilitates navigation and access to content.
4. E-Learning Trends:
- Align the design with the latest trends in e-learning academies.
- Use modern and attractive visual elements.

Key elements:
1. Logo and Brand:
- Design a logo that combines elements related to education and technology.
- Use colors that inspire confidence and energy.
2. Mobile Interface:
- Create a clean and easy-to-use interface.
- Highlight content categories (design, development, marketing) clearly.
3. Iconography and Graphics:
- Use icons and graphics that represent key concepts (screens, books, codes, etc.).
- Maintain a consistent color palette throughout the design.
4. User Experience:
- Prioritize usability and intuitive navigation.
- Include a reviews and testimonials section to highlight the quality of the instructors and content.

Key message:
- "Learn, grow and achieve your goals with Lessonnette."

Future of the project:
- Consider expanding to other platforms (web, tablets) and integrating collaboration and progress tracking tools.

The software used in the layout was Figma.
