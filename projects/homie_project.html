Homie Project

Web Design | UX/UI Design | Interface Design | Mockups & Wireframes

Homie Project is an interface project for a real estate app that offers houses and rooms for all tastes and budgets.

General information:
- Project Name: Homie
- Platform Type: Real estate web portal.
- Philosophy: Following the line of similar applications such as AirBnB or Idealista.
- Global Reach: Properties spread across Europe, America and Asia.
- Focus: Meet the needs of a 21st century home and inhabitants with varied characteristics.

Project objectives:
1. Easy Access to Properties:
- Create an intuitive platform where users can find houses and rooms according to their preferences.
- Facilitate the search and selection of properties.
2. Variety of Options:
- Offer a wide range of properties for all tastes and budgets.
- Includes purchase and rental options.
3. Global Experience:
- Homie must be accessible from anywhere in the world.
- Properties on different continents to serve a diverse audience.
4. Adaptation to the 21st Century:
- Modern and functional design.
- Technology integration for a seamless experience.

Platform content:
1. Property Search:
- Search bar to filter by location, property type, price, etc.
- Results ordered by relevance and proximity.
2. Property Details:
- Individual pages for each property with:
- Images and description.
- Information about rooms, bathrooms, common areas.
- Price and contact details.
3. User Profile:
- User registration to save searches and favorites.
- History of properties visited.
4. Advice and Support:
- Online chat for queries.
- Legal and financial advice.

Design and style:
- Modern and attractive.
- Fresh and clean colors.
- Responsive design for mobile devices and computers.

Value added:
- Quality of Life: Homie not only offers properties, but also a place where people can build their home and improve their quality of life.

The app used to layout this interface was Figma and the icon pack used was Phosphor.
